About

The Quopi AI trader is a software program that uses advanced algorithms and trading analysis tools to automatically execute trades on behalf of the user. The bot has the ability to analyze market conditions and identify profitable trades, based on a combination of technical indicators and market data insights. The bot is designed to continuously monitor the markets, and when it detects a potential trade opportunity, it will automatically execute the trade on the user's behalf.

The Quopi AI Trader is designed to work in the stock market. It is currently capable of trading individual stocks from a predetermined list of options. The stock market is generally very liquid, which means it is easy to buy and sell stocks without significant price impacts. Additionally, publicly traded companies are required to disclose financial information on a regular basis, which can make it easier for us to obtain relevant information for the bot to analyze.

Quopi AI Trader is fully built through the use of a proprietary, custom Generative Pre-trained Transformer which was trained with more than 100,000 pages of content on the topic of trading and related subjects such as books, articles, case studies, even transcripts from conference keynotes. This allows our Artificial Intelligence model a level of expertise realistically unattainable by a human being.

The Quopi Day Trader uses 15 different trading analysis tools. These tools are designed to help the bot analyze market conditions and identify profitable trades. These tools include, but are not limited to technical indicators like RSI, MACD, Moving Averages, Bollinger bands, Fibonacci retracement and support and resistance levels.
