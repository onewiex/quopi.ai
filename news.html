20.02.2023

Why Quopi AI Trader is Inactive Today.

Our trading bot is not active today, despite it being a Monday. We are here to clarify that the reason behind the bot's inactivity is due to a federal holiday in the United States - Presidents' Day.

Presidents Day is observed on the third Monday of February each year, and it honors the birthdays of two former American presidents, George Washington and Abraham Lincoln. On this day, the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) remains closed, which means that there is no trading activity on the exchange.

As our trading bot is designed to operate on the NYSE, it is unable to carry out any trades on Presidents' Day. We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused and want to assure our investors that the bot will resume trading as usual on the next business day.

Sticking to Eastern time trading time. Explained.

The Quopi AI trading bot is designed to only operate during specific times, from 9:30 AM to 4:00 PM Eastern Time. During these hours, the bot will only trade on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) and the NASDAQ. Quopi AI Bot- Beta Testing Accomplished.
